Yetziv Pitgam (יציב פתגם)

Size: 8" x 10"
Completed: 1997

I wrote this manuscript with Pelikan 4001 black ink and an Osmiroid calligraphy pen. I used Daler Rowney Series A and B gouache to color the border design.

The manuscript text was taken from a Jewish liturgical poem that is recited on the second day of Shavuot (Pentecost).
