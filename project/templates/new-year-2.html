New Year Greeting II

Size: 8" x 10"
Completed: 2003

Apart from the black ink border, this card was executed entirely in color pencil. Compared to some inferior brands of color pencils I had the misfortune to use in the past, the Berol PrismaColor pencils I used for this card were of exceptional quality. The lettering has a metallic silver color that is not readily apparent in the scanned image. The verse on the banner is from Psalms 81:4.
