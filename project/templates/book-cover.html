Book Cover

Size: 6" x 8"
Completed: 2000

I designed this frontpiece on commission for a collection of short stories.

I sketched the design with an HB pencil and pounced the drawing onto a clean piece of Bristol paper using tracing paper and a 5B pencil. I inked the design using a Stanford Uniball Micro pen, scanned it, and touched up the image using Gimp.
