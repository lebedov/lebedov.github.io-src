New Year Greeting I

Size: 8" x 10"
Completed: 2000

I drew this rather large card with the intent to create a reasonably detailed scanned image to duplicate and send to several friends. I outlined the letters in pencil and inked them in blue and black ink with a dip pen. The Hebrew words translate to "May you be inscribed for a good year."
